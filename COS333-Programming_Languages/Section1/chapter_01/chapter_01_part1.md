### Notes:
A common test or exam question that you can expect related to these reasons will take the form of a short scenario that I describe. This scenario will involve an individual, and you will have to provide a reason for why this individual needs to know about general programming language concepts. It is important that the reason you provide is relevant to the individual described.

For example, the scenario may involve a manager in a software development company. In this scenario, the first, third, fourth, and fifth reasons are unlikely to be relevant, because a manager will not be actively programming. The second and last reasons are applicable, because a manager might decide on a language to use for a project, and decisions like this will steer the field of computing in some way.
