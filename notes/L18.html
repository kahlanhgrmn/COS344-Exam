Visualization

Computer-generated images that are used to help people

understand both spatial and non-spatial data.

  • Visualisation can be used by humans to make judgments or

decisions.

  • Visualisation allows humans to offload cognition to the

perceptual system, using carefully constructed images as a

form of “external memory”.

  • After computer games, visualisation is one of the biggest uses

of computer graphics.
