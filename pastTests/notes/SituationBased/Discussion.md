

**Group forming Instructions**
 1. For the next 5 minutes, quickly discuss in your **home group** how you will approach your assigned idea.
 2. For 10 minutes, the people with the same topic will form a group, called the **expert group**, and discuss the different approaches for their topic, the drawbacks and benefits and for each approach, what will work the best, and where it can be used.
 3. For 10 minutes after this, in your home groups, discuss how the entire scene will be rendered, how each topic will be integrated and the reasoning behind your choices.
 4. Each home group will then get 4 minutes where they will explain to the other home groups how they would approach the render, and the other groups will get to ask questions.
 5. Lastly, for 5 minutes, you must critique the attempt at answering the question.
