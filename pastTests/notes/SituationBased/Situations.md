
**Scenario 1: Deep Sea Exploration**
 * Render submarines navigating deep trenches with robotic arms collecting samples and bioluminescent flora.
 * Wildlife: giant squids and glowing jellyfish.
 * Cameras: ROV attached lenses and submersible dash cams.
 * Movement: swimming creatures and propellers pushing through water currents.

**Scenario 2: Medieval Castle Siege**
 * Render a castle with soldiers, siege weapons, and animated fire effects.
 * Wildlife equivalent: horses and birds.
 * Cameras: overhead and wall-mounted.
 * Movement: soldiers marching and catapults firing.

**Scenario 3: Martian Colony Habitat**
 * Render a sprawling red desert base with solar panels, hydroponic domes, and swirling dust storms.
 * Wildlife equivalent: survey drones and robotic rovers.
 * Cameras: satellite tracking and helmet-mounted astronaut cams.
 * Movement: rovers rolling on treads and astronauts bounding in low gravity.
